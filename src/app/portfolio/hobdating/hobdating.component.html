Introduction

Hob Dating is my first significant and personal project developed using the Swift, SwiftUI, and Combine frameworks. As an avid Android enthusiast for many years, I never had the opportunity to create my own SaaS. Therefore, Hob Dating serves as a platform for me to enhance my skills in integrating and developing applications.

Design

In the highly competitive dating app market, I recognized the critical need for an innovative and aesthetically captivating design to distinguish Hob Dating from the multitude of existing platforms. Leveraging Figma, I crafted a comprehensive UI/UX design that prioritizes user intuition and engagement. Every interface element was thoughtfully designed to ensure a seamless and enjoyable user experience, blending functionality with elegant visual appeal. My dedication to creating a standout application is evident in the polished aesthetics and user-centric features of Hob Dating. This project not only showcases my technical proficiency but also my unwavering passion for design and creation, positioning Hob Dating as a unique and attractive solution in the dynamic landscape of online dating.
